Welcome to Intach Family

    INTACH is dedicated to conserving India's heritage. Our mission is eloquently expressed in the INTACH logo that is derived from the 3000-year-old anthropomorphic copper figure of Shahabad, Uttar Pradesh.

    It symbolizes a human figure with arms turned inwards, suggesting that we hold the future of earth in our hands. We need to contemplate the direction we should take: conservation of inheritance or consumption.

    It is the human genius that has created our diverse composite arts and cultural heritage over the centuries. Therefore, we believe that heritage enhances the quality of life and we must preserve it for future generation to enjoy.

    INTACH is an organization of volunteers who are deeply committed to conserving, and enhancing India's diverse cultural heritage.

INTACH was established in 1984 with the mission and mandate enshrined in the Memorandum of Association that encompasses a holistic understanding of:

  • Built (architectural) heritage
  • Natural heritage
  • Art (material) heritage
  • Living (intangible) heritage

    INTACH's principal strength lies in its Chapters that form a wide network throughout the country. The Chapter Conveners facilitate action at the local level. The core professional staff at the Central Office support Chapter initiatives.
